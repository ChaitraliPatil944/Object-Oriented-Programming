# 7. Preparation Tips

- Understand concepts instead of memorizing.
- Practice writing code.
- Explain concepts aloud.
- Review common design patterns.
- Solve scenario-based questions.

Because interviewers eventually ask "why?" after every answer. Nature's way of detecting memorized definitions.
